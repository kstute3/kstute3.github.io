About Me

Hi there, my name is Katie Stute, and I am a student at the University of Miami. I am nineteen years old and I am from Darien, Connecticut. I am passionate about graphic design, and enjoy dance. In my free time, you can find me hanging with friends at the beach. I am a hardworking and organized person.

At the University of Miami, I am studying:

  • Media Management
  • Advertising
  • Marketing

Aside from classes, I work for my school's newspaper, The Hurricane, as the editor of layout. I am also a part of Zeta Tau Alpha, a social sorority on campus.

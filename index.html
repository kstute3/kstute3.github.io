About Me: Katie Stute

My name is Katie Stute. I am nineteen years old and I am from Darien, Connecticut. Now I live in Coral Gables, Miami for college. I am a current Freshman at the University of Miami. Some of my favorite things are my family, friends, dance, food and travel.

School Life

Last year I graduated from St. Luke’s School in New Canaan, Connecticut. I went to St. Luke’s for 8 years, for both middle and high school. I was the editor of my school's yearbook, head senior tour guide, head of the Party Planning Committee, and head of marketing and social media for the Alumni Committee.

Today, I am in the honors Communication School at the University of Miami. As well, I am a Foote Fellow. I am majoring in Media Management with minors in Advertising and Marketing. I work for the Hurricane newspaper as the cohead of layout and design. I recently joined a campus sorority, Zeta Tau Alpha.

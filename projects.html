Independent Study Magazine Spreads

I presented this project after a year of working on it to the faculty at St. Luke's in New Canaan, Ct. After seeing this presentation, I got an internship with the marketing and communications team for this upcoming summer, 2023.

One of my favorite things that I have done is make magazine spreads. In my senior year of high school I made an independent study where I made a magazine for my high school. I learned about InDesign and the rules of layout. Once I learned the rules of layout, was when I started to break these rules and make the spreads more visually appealing.

Travel Campaign

In my first semester at UMiami, I took introduction to graphic design. I was given the task of making a travel campign for a specific expierience in a location. We had to make a poster, billboard and brochure. These were made up of a compellation of multiple images blended with one another.

My campaign was selling a sailing experience in Santorini, Greece. The poster and billboard were made using Adobe Photoshop and the brochure was made using Adobe InDesign.
